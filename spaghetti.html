Simple Spaghetti

This is a simple no frills spaghetti recipe for an easy dinner

If you enjoy uncomplicated recipes this is the spaghetti dish for you! No unnecessary ingredients or extra steps just boiled noodles, browned hamburger, and a sauce of your choice. spaghetti cannot get any more simple.

Ingredients

  • hamburger meat
  • spaghetti/pasta/tomato sauce
  • noodles

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Brown hamburger
  3. After browning hamburger mix in sauce
  4. Bring to low heat
  5. Once noodles are done pour off water and mix noodles in with meat sauce
