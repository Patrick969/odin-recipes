Lemon Pepper Salmon

This is a quick and easy to clean up meal

This delicious home cooked dinner can be prepped, cooked and cleaned in under 30 minutes if done correctly. There are no dishes to wash, only nutrious fishes to eat.

Ingredients

  • salmon patty
  • butter
  • lemon or lemon juice
  • red pepper
  • black pepper
  • oregano
  • garlic

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. melt butter in microwave safe bowl
  3. add lemon juice to butter
  4. place salmon onto sheets of aluminum foil
  5. paint lemon-butter onto salmon
  6. sprinkle on garlic, red pepper, black pepper, oregano, and salt.
  7. Wrap salmon in aluminum foil
  8. place in oven for 20-25 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
