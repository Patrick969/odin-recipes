Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

The venerable peanut butter and jelly sandwich

The PBJ is the epitome of needing something to eat and not wanting to cook anything. It's what your mom made you as a kid and what you'll make for your kids. It's the official sandwich of nostalgia. The classic sandwich captures youthful vigor with sugary sweetness while exuding adult laziness with crude simplicity.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • fruit jelly

steps

  1. prep bread for smearing
  2. smear some peanut butter
  3. smear some jelly
  4. it's peanut butter jelly time!
